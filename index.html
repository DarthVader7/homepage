I am a currently pursuing an M. Phil degree in Machine Learning Speech and Language Technologies at University of Cambridge.

Before arriving at Cambridge, I finished an undergraduate degree in Mathematics and Computing at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi.

I like to learn, wonder about and explore a little bit of anything and everything. My current research interests span the broad area of Machine Learning. More specifically I am interested in investigating statistical techniques which lead to easier generalization of models for scalability. I am also interested in applications of machine learning in vision and speech.
