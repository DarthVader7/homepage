I am a currently pursuing an M. Phil degree in Machine Learning Speech and Language Technologies at University of Cambridge. Before arriving at Cambridge, I finished an undergraduate degree in Mathematics and Computing at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi.

I like to think, learn, wonder about and explore a little bit of anything and everything. However, my current interests span over the broad area of statistical machine learning and its applications. In my embryonic stints as an undergrad, I was introduced to a wide-breadth of challenges solved using computer science and engineering. Through other forays and activities, I also enjoy some fun hobbies and interests.
